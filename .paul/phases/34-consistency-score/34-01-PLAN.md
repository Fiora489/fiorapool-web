<objective>
## Goal
Build dedicated `/progression/consistency` page with a composite Consistency Score (0–100), breakdown of contributing factors (KDA stability, CS stability, win stability, session regularity), and a 6-point trend line over the last 12 weeks (bi-weekly rolling window).

## Purpose
Players who want to improve need to know whether their results are stable or erratic. This page answers "are you playing consistently, or is your performance bouncing around?" with a single number + factor breakdown.
</objective>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Build src/lib/consistency.ts</name>
  <files>src/lib/consistency.ts</files>
  <action>
    New module. Score formula: 100 - normalised variance, weighted across 4 factors.

    Factors (each 0-100, where 100 = most consistent):
    1. kdaStability — based on coefficient of variation (CV = stddev/mean) of per-match KDA across last 30 games. Lower CV = higher score. Map: CV=0→100, CV=0.5→75, CV=1.0→50, CV=1.5→25, CV>=2.0→0 (linear interpolation).
    2. csStability — same CV method on CS/min across last 30 games
    3. winStability — measure of how clustered wins/losses are. Compute longest win streak AND longest loss streak; score = 100 - (maxStreakOrLossStreak - 3) × 10 (clamped 0-100). Long streaks either way = less consistent.
    4. sessionRegularity — unique days played in last 30 days (not last-30-games). Score = (daysPlayed / 30) × 100 clamped to 100.

    Weights: kda 30%, cs 25%, win 25%, session 20%.

    Overall score = weighted average of factor scores.

    Tier: <40 Volatile / 40-59 Erratic / 60-79 Steady / 80+ Rock Solid

    Verdict per factor: 'excellent' (>=80), 'good' (60-79), 'needs work' (40-59), 'poor' (<40)

    Trend: 6 bi-weekly buckets over last 12 weeks. Each bucket computes score from matches in that 2-week window (or null if <5 matches in window).

    Return full payload with score, tier, factors[], trend[], lowConfidence flag if <10 matches total.
  </action>
