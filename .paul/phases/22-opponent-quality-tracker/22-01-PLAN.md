<objective>
## Goal
Turn `/analytics/opponent-quality` into a real opponent matchup dashboard: overview (diversity, total opponents, avg WR), best/worst matchups (≥3 game threshold), lane-phase performance per enemy (avg gold@10, cs@10), and a sortable full opponent table.

## Purpose
The original feature name implies MMR-based opponent quality, but the schema has no per-match opponent rank field. Pivoting scope to **opponent matchup quality** — same data, more useful framing: "which enemies do I struggle against, and how badly?"
