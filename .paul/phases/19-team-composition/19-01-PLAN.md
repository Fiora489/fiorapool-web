<objective>
## Goal
Turn `/analytics/team-comp` into a proper archetype analysis page: show your per-archetype performance (games, win rate, avg KDA), your strongest/weakest archetype, the archetype-vs-archetype matchup grid (you vs enemy laner archetype), and per-archetype champion breakdown.

## Purpose
The current page only shows archetype counts with bars — no performance signal. To coach composition choices, the page needs to answer: "Which archetype do I actually win with, and against which enemy archetypes?"

## Output
- Enhanced `computeTeamComp()` returning archetype performance matrix, strongest/weakest archetype, matchup grid, and champions grouped by archetype
- Rewritten team-comp page with 4 sections (overview, matrix, matchup grid, champion breakdown)
- 3 new components
</objective>
