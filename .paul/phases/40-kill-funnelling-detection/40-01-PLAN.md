    - 'recipient' (funnel target): kills ≥ 10 AND kills ≥ assists × 1.5

    Over all matches, count each classification. Compute WR for each.

    Profile label:
    - If recipient share ≥ 50% → 'carry'
    - If provider share ≥ 50% → 'support'
    - If both categories collectively less than 30% → 'balanced'
    - Otherwise 'mixed'

    Per-champion lists:
    - topRecipientChampions: top 5 champs where you were recipient most
