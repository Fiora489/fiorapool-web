<objective>
## Goal
Build dedicated `/coaching/momentum` page — a momentum index (hot / neutral / cold / tilt), rolling 5-game win rate curve over last 20 games, last-20 W/L grid, and a preview of how the next game's result would shift momentum.

## Purpose
Players want an at-a-glance read on "am I on a hot streak or tilting right now?" — this page gives that plus a tilt warning when in a rough patch.
</objective>
