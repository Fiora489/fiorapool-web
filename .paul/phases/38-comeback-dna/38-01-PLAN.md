    Deficit buckets:
    - 'slight':      gold_diff_at_10 in (-1500, -500]

    - 'disaster':    gold_diff_at_10 ≤ -3000

    For each bucket: games, wins, winRate.

    Overall behind: all games where gold_diff_at_10 < -500 (inclusive of all 3 buckets).

    Score formula (0-100):
    - 0% behind WR → 0 score
    - 25% behind WR → 50 (baseline)
    - 50%+ behind WR → 100
    Linear interpolation.

    Tier: <25 Fragile / 25-49 Developing / 50-74 Resilient / 75+ Unstoppable

    champions: group comeback wins (win + gold@10 ≤ -500) by champion, top 5 by count.

    traits:
    - avgComebackDuration (minutes): avg game_duration_seconds / 60 of comeback wins
    - avgCsRecovery: avg (cs_diff_at_20 - cs_diff_at_10) of comeback wins when both cs_diffs present (positive = recovered CS lead)
    - avgMomentumSwing: avg abs(gold_diff_at_10) of comeback wins (how deep the deficit was)
